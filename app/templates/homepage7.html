About Us


With Talentdisha, we bring a platform where jobseekers can find their ideal career and recruitments can find the job aspirant that fit perfect to their job descriptions.


What’s different about us is that we are curating an exclusive “Learn and Mentor” program that connects freshers to the experienced pros in their fields to avail them with the right help for pursuing their passion! And we also provide “Training and Personal Development” for freshers to brush up their skills and dexterity in their career and get the dream job they are striving for!
